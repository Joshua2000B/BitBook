About BitBook

BitBook is library database with an interface to allow users to purchase books created by 6 Computer Science students at Kent State University. We are in our early stages of construction so not all of the desired features are available. We would like to thank Unsplash for the image from https://unsplash.com/images/things/book. Thank you for using our site!
